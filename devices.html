SmartCane

Role: Interaction & enclosure design (2024)

A white-cane attachment that adds haptic feedback for obstacles above waist height.

  • Ultrasonic range-finding up to 150 cm.
  • Quiet vibration motor → does not draw attention in public.
  • Single-button interface for *eyes-free* mode switching.
